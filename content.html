STEPHEN CURRY

Stephen curry graduated from high school and joined Davidson College.

Freshman season Curry averaging 21.5 points in the field, became the best rookie and scorer of the SEC league.

Sophomore season, curry led the Davidson team into the NCAA Championship quarterfinals.

In the third season, curry completed the transition from attack guard to point guard who led the nation with 28.6 points per game
